Data Scientist - Market Analysis and Learning Resources

Data Scientists extract insights from data to drive business decisions. They combine statistics, programming, and domain expertise.

Role Overview:
Data scientists work across industries analyzing business problems and building predictive models. They communicate findings to stakeholders and translate requirements into analytics solutions.

Key Activities:
- Exploratory data analysis and visualization
- Statistical hypothesis testing
- Building predictive models
- Data storytelling and presentation
- SQL and database queries
- Python for data manipulation (Pandas, NumPy)
- Visualization tools (Tableau, Power BI, Matplotlib)

Market Insights 2026:
- 25% growth in data science roles year-over-year
- Increased demand for AI/ML knowledge
- Privacy-preserving analytics becoming standard
- Generative AI for synthetic data creation
- Emphasis on AI ethics and bias detection
